BUDGET BUDDY

Data for your decisions

Budget Buddy is a website that will be a service to anyone who wants to manage their money. Anybody will be able to track their expenses they have each month and create a budget. This will make them more aware of their spending habits and help them turn over a new leaf in life as they will grow better in their walk with money.

Unlock your potential with Buddy Breakdown. A feature that allows members to see where all of their money is going! Knowing where your money goes plays a huge part into how you spend your money. A personal pie chart can show a user what categories their money is going into and how much is going to each one. This is a great way to see where most of your money ends up. Not happy about it? Let us talking about budgeting!
