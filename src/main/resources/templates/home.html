LAYING IT ALL OUT

Budget Buddy

Our goal is to help you grow in your own walk with money. One way we can do this is through talking about budgeting. Budgeting is a great way to tell yourself where money should be going each month, but it is only up to you to hold yourself accountable to that budget. Just because something is on sale or clearance, does not mean that you can afford it in this month's budget. We provide an easy way for members that can help them plan out where money should be going each month. Maybe even help them start to stash a little something on the side!
